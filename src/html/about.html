About Sporthorse Revival

Sporthorse Revival is a personal account of Cat Allen's experience in the equestrian industry that led her to her current mission of championing the responsible ownership of American Thoroughbreds. She hopes that her writing will light a path toward a more equitable, inclusive, and humane sport that will inspire the next generation of equestrians to think and ride different.
