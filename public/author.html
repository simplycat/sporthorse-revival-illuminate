  • Cat Allen
  • Cat Allen grew up riding in the suburbs of Chicago. In her early years, she began as an eventer - learning the ropes of dressage, cross country, and stadium jumping at Frontenac Farm. During the summer, you could find her practically living at the barn at Cedar Lodge - a hunter/jumper focused summer camp. For seven consecutive summers of nearly two month stints at a time, there she worked with amazing trainers every day, twice a day (and sometimes three times a day). They took her to her first horse show on a little red Arabian crossbred mare named Jasmine, where they took first place in their very first class. In high school, Cat worked part-time at retail and food establishments to spend every last penny on shareboarding a big-bodied Thoroughbred named Bubba at Pingree Oaks Farm. After graduating a year early from high school, Cat moved to South Carolina to ride under Collins Daye at Irish Oaks Equestrian Center and coach of the University of South Carolina IHSA team at the time. She later moved to Virginia to ride Off-the-Track Thoroughbreds (OTTBs) when she fell in love with a small college in the mountains of Virginia. She worked at two different top AA/A barns in Chicagoland riding, teaching and training horses and humans of all ages and levels. She also spent a year as a working student under a Polish dressage trainer, Agata Rakucka, to hone her flat skills - but realized jumping was her calling. Once Cat graduated, she worked for a Sponsorships and Events Coordinator for a non-profit tied to the equestrian industry. She was responsible for planning a gala on a private equestrian estate, hosting the world's top riders, to raise money to fund life-saving programs for children in underprivileged countries. After a series of serendipitous events, Cat quickly found herself back in the saddle riding as well as barn managing for private operations and later a large-scale breeding and training operation. After Cat suffered a life-altering injury, she took a brief hiatus from riding before spending two years rehabbing her mind, body, and soul through volunteering at a hippotherapy and equine-assisted therapy operation. Today, Cat is enjoying life as an amateur, writing her story as she lives it. She has since shifted gears into a lucrative career in Cybersecurity, with a focus in network security. Cat's ultimate goal is to find her path, alongside her partner Unbridled Dixie, to return to the jumper arena. She aspires to one day wear the coveted scarlet red coat and represent the United States on the world stage.
Cat Allen
Founder & CTO, Doberman Digital
